# Future Research Directions — DEAI Pipeline

**From methodological scaffold to validated population science**

---

## Immediate Next Steps (6–12 months)

### Study 1: Empirical Validation in LASI Wave 1
- **Design:** Cross-sectional validation of DEAI in LASI (N≈72,000, India, ≥45yr)
- **Key additions:** Individual-level linkage; longitudinal LASI Wave 2 for mortality outcome
- **Expected contribution:** AUC, NRI, calibration curves with real frailty and disability data
- **Data access:** https://lasida.iips.in/ (free researcher registration)

### Study 2: DHS Cross-Country Validation
- **Design:** DEAI validation across 5–10 DHS countries (Kenya, Bangladesh, Peru, Nigeria, India)
- **Objective:** Test whether DEAI generalises across LMIC contexts
- **Key variable:** Does environmental DEAI domain (PM2.5, heat) vary in importance by country?

---

## Medium-Term Studies (1–3 years)

### Study 3: Longitudinal DEAI and Ageing Trajectory
- **Design:** Prospective cohort study with 5-year follow-up
- **New outcome:** Rate of frailty index change (trajectory slope)
- **Key question:** Does higher baseline DEAI predict steeper ageing trajectory?

### Study 4: Molecular DEAI Validation (Multi-modal Integration)
- **Design:** Subset of LASI or new cohort with blood samples
- **Assays:** DNA methylation (EPIC array), inflammation panel (CRP, IL-6, TNF-α), telomere length
- **Key question:** Does DEAI correlate with epigenetic clock acceleration (GrimAge, DunedinPACE)?

### Study 5: DEAI Response to Intervention
- **Design:** Randomised trial or natural experiment
- **Intervention:** Clean cooking fuel provision / tobacco cessation programme
- **Outcome:** Change in DEAI and frailty index at 12 months
- **Key question:** Is DEAI sensitive to short-term exposome change?

---

## Long-Term Vision (3–5 years)

### Study 6: DEAI as a Screening Tool in Primary Care
- **Design:** Implementation study in 50 primary health centres (India)
- **Protocol:** DEAI calculated from routine health worker survey (15-item questionnaire)
- **Intervention:** DEAI-guided preventive care package (nutrition + air quality counselling)
- **Outcome:** Frailty-free life years at 3 years

### Study 7: DEAI for Health Policy Simulation
- **Design:** Microsimulation (using LASI cohort)
- **Question:** If India reduces PM2.5 to WHO guidelines, what is population-level DEAI and frailty reduction?
- **Tools:** DEAI coefficients as input to health impact model
