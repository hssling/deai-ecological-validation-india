# MJDRDYPU Target Rationale

## Recommended first Indian journal
Medical Journal of Dr. D.Y. Patil Vidyapeeth (MJDRDYPU)

## Why this journal fits the current manuscript
- The current add-on is a narrow, peer-reviewed, human biomarker meta-analysis rather than a large clinical-effect meta-analysis.
- The manuscript is methodologically conservative and avoids overclaiming age reversal.
- The article is readable for a general medical audience and does not depend on specialist geroscience framing alone.
- MJDRDYPU publishes review articles and systematic-review style manuscripts and is a more realistic Indian first target for the present evidence base than a more selective national flagship journal.

## Current manuscript positioning for this journal
- Article type: Review article / systematic review with quantitative synthesis
- Best framing: human anti-ageing intervention evidence on DNA methylation biomarkers
- Avoided framing: proof of age reversal, definitive clinical rejuvenation, broad meta-analysis of all anti-ageing interventions

## Core message for editorial positioning
This manuscript offers a conservative, clinically readable quantitative synthesis of peer-reviewed human intervention evidence on DNA methylation biomarkers of biological ageing. The pooled evidence is intentionally narrow, restricted to directly extractable comparator-based data, and is framed as biomarker modulation rather than proof of rejuvenation.

## Submission caveat
- The current package is suitable as a focused review/meta-analytic add-on.
- The manuscript should not be submitted as a broad or definitive anti-ageing treatment meta-analysis.
- Final author scientific sign-off remains necessary before external submission.
