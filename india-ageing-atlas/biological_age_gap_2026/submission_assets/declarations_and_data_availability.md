# Declarations (Discover Aging)

**Ethics approval and consent to participate.** This study is a secondary analysis of de-identified
data from the Longitudinal Ageing Study in India (LASI) Wave 1, obtained under provider access
conditions. The original LASI protocol received ethics approval from the Indian Council of Medical
Research and institutional review boards of the partner institutions, and all original participants
gave written informed consent. As a secondary analysis of de-identified data, the present study
involved no new contact with participants; an institutional ethics determination/waiver will be
provided on request by the journal.

**Consent for publication.** Not applicable; no identifiable individual data are presented.

**Competing interests.** The author declares no competing interests.

# Data availability statement

The individual-level LASI Wave 1 microdata (Gateway to Global Aging harmonized files and the IIPS
dried-blood-spot and spirometry datasets) are governed by the data providers' access conditions and
cannot be redistributed by the author. They are available to qualified researchers on application to
the Gateway to Global Aging Data (https://g2aging.org) and the International Institute for Population
Sciences (https://www.iipsindia.ac.in/lasi). The analysis code that reproduces every table and figure
from these source files, the variable-construction specification, and aggregate (non-disclosive)
output tables are available from the author on reasonable request and will be deposited in a public
repository (e.g., Zenodo/GitHub) on acceptance, with a DOI cited here.
